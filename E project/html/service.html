Our Therapy

  • Behaviour Therapy

    Behavioral therapy is an umbrella term for types of therapy that treat mental health disorders.

  • Humainistic Therapy

    A mental health approach that emphasizes the importance of being your true self in order to lead the most fulfilling life.

  • Trauma Therapy

    Therapy that recognize understanding how the traumatic experience impacts a child's mental,behavioral,and spiritual well-being.

  • Cognitive Therapy

    Short-term form of psychotherapy based on the concept that the way we think about things affects how we feel emotionally.

  • Holistic Therapy

    Type of therapy that address the “whole” person. Its goal is to help individuals develop a deeper to understad themselves

  • Psycodynamic Therapy

    An approach that involves facilitation a deeper understanding of one's emotions and other mental processes.
